AWARENESS: The awareness of endangerments have caused major reform in the way exotic animals are protected.

  • By enforcing strict laws and adding layers of protection against endangered animals, Nepal has lowered their precentage of poaching down to zero. Not a single rhino, elephant or tiger has been killed.
  • U.N. Government has created court orders that hault Japan from Antarctic Whaling. This was caused by a direct corrulation in the loss of whale populations from comerical and overfishing.
  • In an effort to protect wildlife, California banned hunting with guns and ammunition.
  • President Barack Obama has launched many major policies to cut back on pollution, establish parks to protect animals and ban the exposure of harmful elements to wildlife.
  • Countries enforce many laws to protect certain animals so they have a chance to recover from being endangered. Puerto Rico created a law to protect leatherback turtles and gorillas are protected in New Congo National Parks.
  • Pesticides have been banned in the U.K. in response to the bee scare and chemical runoff.
  • France, Australia and South Korea have established shark sanctuaries and banned the selling and transportation of shark fins.

The amount of progress humans have made to reverse the effects of poaching and killing is incredible. Everyday, more and more stories appear of animal rights and repopulations.
